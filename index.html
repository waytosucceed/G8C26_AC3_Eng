Adverbs


Key concepts

  • Based on the kind of sense they are making sentences can be divided into:

    • Declarative/Assertive, Interrogative, Exclamatory, Imperative, Optative

  • All the above types of sentences can be further divided into Affirmative/Positive and Negative.
  • Depending on the context a single word can also be treated as a sentence.

New Words!

Exasperated: (adjective) intensely irritated and frustrated.
